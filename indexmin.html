Friday, 5 August, 2016

As much as I hated the busy world of London estate agency, with its nightmarish characters and supposedly busy marketplace, I can at least thank it for giving me the shove needed to look at a career change. Property buying agents are the new Von Dutch caps (remember those?), hideously expensive but increasingly popular. I was aghast while researching these agents' online presence. Well over 30% had no online presence at all, while a significant number had a very outdated website. In a market where applicants are more reluctant to view properties and more inclined to increase their desktop searches, it is more important than ever that these agents provide a sleek and informative service. Its either modernize or risk losing the already decreasing number of active clients in the prime London market.
